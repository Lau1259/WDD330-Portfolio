Get Inspired

If you're losing the will to keep up with your goals you might need a little inspiration to keep going.

Motivational Sources

A large part of staying motivated is surrounding yourself with things that help you keep your eye on the prize. This can be people, songs, sticky notes or anything else that reminds you of why you're trying to change and grow.

Quotes

One source of help can be motivational quotes. They help you remember your focus and can become your mantra.

Write your purpose

Another source of help can be a written reminder. Why are you doing this? What do you want to accomplish? Sometimes the best way to remember why you need to keep going is to have a list of reasons written down (typed). We'll get you started with the first one.
